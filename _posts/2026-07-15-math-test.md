Here is some inline math: $f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{f}(\xi) e^{2 \pi i x \xi} d\xi$ to show Fourier transforms in standard text flow.

And here is a display equation centered on its own line:

$$\mathbf{A} = \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix}$$

You can even write complex logic:

$$
\lim_{n \to \infty} \left( 1 + \frac{1}{n} \right)^n = e
$$
